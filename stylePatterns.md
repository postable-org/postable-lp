Design a complete visual identity system for a SaaS product called "Postable" — an AI-powered social media automation tool for small businesses.

The brand aesthetic is strictly black and white — monochrome, minimal, and calm. No accent colors anywhere in the UI. The interface should feel like a premium, editorial-quality tool — think Notion's restraint, Linear's precision, and Stripe's professionalism combined.

Color system:
- Primary text: #0A0A0A (near-black)
- Secondary text: #6B6B6B (mid gray)  
- Muted/placeholder text: #B0B0B0 (light gray)
- Borders and dividers: #E0E0E0
- Card/surface backgrounds: #F5F5F5 (off-white)
- Page background: #FFFFFF (pure white)
- Dark mode inverts this scale — white text on near-black backgrounds

There are NO accent colors in the UI. Buttons are black with white text. Links are black underlined. Active states use darker black or lighter gray. The only color in the entire product is the logo mark.

Typography: DM Sans (Google Fonts) for headings and UI, Inter for body text. Two weights dominate — Regular 400 for body, Bold 700 for headings. Clean, generous spacing. Base unit 4px, scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64.

Border radius: 8px for buttons and inputs, 12px for cards, 16px for modals and large containers. Shadows are barely visible — 0 1px 2px rgba(0,0,0,0.06) maximum.

The overall feeling: a tool so quiet and confident that the content it creates becomes the star. Black and white is not boring — it's intentional restraint that makes the user's colorful social media posts pop by contrast.

Deliver: color palette sheet, typography scale, spacing system, component samples (buttons, inputs, cards, navigation) all in this monochrome system.
